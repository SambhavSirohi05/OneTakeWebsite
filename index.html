User Guide

Six steps,
one recording.

06 STEPS
1
Required
Select display & audio source
Pick your recording monitor from the Display dropdown — all connected displays appear automatically. Set Audio to Screen, Mic, Both, or None. Choose output resolution and a save folder.
2
Optional
Configure Layout Settings
Open Layout Settings to set your presentation canvas background, layout padding, border corners, shadows, camera bubble size, shape, corner, cursor zoom strength, idle delay, and overlay opacity.
3
Optional
Load your script
Paste your script into the built-in editor in Layout Settings. Click "Show Script" — the teleprompter appears orange-highlighted in the control panel. Drag it directly in front of your camera lens.
4
Required
Hit Start Recording
Click the blue button. Teleprompter scrolling begins after 3 seconds. Cursor spotlight is suppressed for 4 seconds. Record your content — OneTake handles everything live.
5
Required
Stop and wait
Click Stop. OneTake composites the camera bubble at full resolution and blends all audio tracks into a single stereo output. Your file is saved to the chosen directory.
6
Optional
Add AI subtitles
Trigger the Sarvam AI transcription flow. A progress modal shows live status. Pick your subtitle style, color, and opacity — then export the final subtitled video.
